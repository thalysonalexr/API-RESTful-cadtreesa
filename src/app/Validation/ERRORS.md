	2 - field is blank
	3 - Address email invalid
	4 - Field type string
	5 - Length error
	6 - Invalid CPF
	7 - Invalid RGA
	8 - RGA or CPF
	9 - Field type float
	10 - Field type int
	11 - Field type enum
	12 - Email exists
	13 - RGACPFs exists
	14 - Url invalid
	15 - Invalid amount
	16 - Field not found

## 2 - Not authorized

	1 - User not found
